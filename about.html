hello! My name is Ashrita and I'm currently a junior at Cornell University majoring in computer science and minoring in music and linguistics.

when I'm not coding, you can find me playing violin with the Cornell Symphony Orchestra, practicing piano in a small practice room, eating and taking pictures of good food, drinking overpriced coffee, and hanging out with friends.
