Hello! My name is Ashrita and I'm currently a junior at Cornell University majoring in computer science and minoring in music and linguistics.

My main interests lie in the fields of natural language processing, mobile development, web development, and music technology. I am passionate about the intersection between computer science and the arts and using technology to solve real-world problems.

At Cornell, I am a member of Cornell Design and Tech Initiative, a project team that creates technology for community impact. Currently, I am an iOS developer for eve, an app that allows students to track events across campus and share them with others.

When I'm not coding, you can find me playing violin with the Cornell Symphony Orchestra, practicing piano in a small practice room, eating and taking pictures of good food, and running along the gorges trails of Ithaca.
